# Create Mod Questline × Minecolonies Integration

Worker progression and research integration for Create Mod within minecolonies colonies.

---

## ⚙️ ANDESITE AGE

### Chapter 1 — Rotational Foundations (Tier 1-2)

**Research:** `andesite-age/rotational-foundations`

Workers involved:
- **Blacksmith** (Lv1): Makes Andesite Alloy & Shafts (research gates recipes)
- **Forester** (Lv1): Provides stripped logs and wood materials
- **Carpenter** (Lv2): Assembles wood-based components

Milestones:
1. Craft **Andesite Alloy** *(Andesite + Iron/Zinc Nugget)*
2. Craft a **Shaft** *(from Alloy)* — Blacksmith produces
3. Craft a **Cogwheel** *(Planks + Shaft)* — Player or Carpenter
4. Craft **Engineer's Goggles** *(see stress & speed)*
5. Build a **Water Wheel** *(Planks + Shaft, needs flowing water)*
6. Connect a basic shaft/cogwheel network — learn RPM & Stress Units

### Chapter 2 — Casings & Transport (Tier 3-4)

**Research:** `andesite-age/casings-transport`  
**Requires:** Rotational Foundations research

Workers involved:
- **Blacksmith** (Lv3): Produces mechanical components (press, belts, gears, casings from metal)
- **Forester** (Lv3): Provides stripped logs for casing assembly
- **Carpenter** (Lv3): Assembles wood-based mechanical frames and supports

Milestones:
7. Make **Andesite Casing** *(apply Alloy to a Stripped Log)*
8. Craft a **Mechanical Press** *(Shaft + Andesite Casing + Block of Iron)*
9. Press Iron Ingots into **Iron Sheets** *(needed for belts and more)*
10. Craft a **Mechanical Belt** *(transport items between machines)*

### Chapter 3 — First Processing (Tier 4)

**Research:** `andesite-age/first-processing`  
**Requires:** Casings & Transport research

Workers unlocked:
- **Crusher** (Lv3) — Now can process ores

Workers involved:
- **Crusher** (Lv3): Operates Millstone for ore milling
- **Farmer** (Lv2): Uses Encased Fan for bulk washing automation & wheat processing
- **Forester** (Lv4): Produces more complex Create blocks

Milestones:
11. Craft a **Basin** *(base for Press/Mixer operations)*
12. Craft a **Millstone** *(Cogwheel + Andesite Casing + Stone — basic ore milling)*
13. Mill Zinc Ore to get Zinc Ingots/Nuggets
14. Craft an **Encased Fan** *(Shaft + Andesite Casing + Propeller)*
15. Build a **Bulk Washing** setup *(Fan + Water = free ore doubling from crushed ore)*

### Chapter 4 — Mining Contraptions (Tier 5)

**Research:** `andesite-age/mining-contraptions`  
**Requires:** First Processing research

Workers involved:
- **Miner** (Lv5): Operates Mechanical Drill for automated mining
- **Forester** (Lv5): Operates Mechanical Saw for wood processing
- **Farmer** (Lv4): Operates Mechanical Harvester for crop automation
- **Crusher** (Lv5): Advanced ore processing with Crushing Wheels

Milestones:
16. Craft a **Mechanical Drill** *(automated block mining)*
17. Craft a **Mechanical Saw** *(wood/stone processing)*
18. Build a **Moving Contraption** *(Mechanical Bearing or Piston + Chassis)*
19. Craft a **Mechanical Harvester** *(automate crop farms)*

### Chapter 5 — Bridge to Brass (Tier 5)

**Research:** `andesite-age/bridge-to-brass`  
**Requires:** Mining Contraptions research

Workers unlocked:
- **Librarian** (Lv3) gains research specialty in Create Tech — NEW ABILITY

Workers involved:
- **Librarian** (Lv3): Can research Blaze Burner technology
- **Blacksmith** (Lv5): Produces Mechanical Mixer and prepares for Brass Age
- **Forester** (Lv5): Produces Flywheel components

Milestones:
20. Craft a **Mechanical Mixer** *(Cogwheel + Andesite Casing + Whisk, requires 30 RPM)*
21. Use Mixer to make Andesite Alloy more efficiently
22. **Go to the Nether** — collect Blaze Rods
23. Craft a **Blaze Burner** *(the heat source that unlocks Brass)*

## 🔩 BRASS AGE

### Chapter 6 — Smelting Brass (Tier 6)

**Research:** `brass-age/smelting-brass`  
**Requires:** Bridge to Brass research + Blaze Burner built

Workers involved:
- **Blacksmith** (Lv6): Produces Brass Ingots and casings
- **Smelter** (Lv6): Operates heated mixing recipes with Blaze Burner

Milestones:
24. Mix **Brass Ingots** *(Mixer + heated Blaze Burner + Copper Ingot + Zinc Ingot)*
25. Apply Brass Ingot to a Stripped Log → **Brass Casing**
26. Press Brass into **Brass Sheets**
27. Craft a **Flywheel** *(Brass + Shaft — large stress buffer)*

### Chapter 7 — Electronics & Sorting (Tier 6-7)

**Research:** `brass-age/electronics-sorting`  
**Requires:** Smelting Brass research

Workers involved:
- **Mechanic** (Lv6): Produces Electron Tubes (precision electronics)
- **Blacksmith** (Lv7): Produces Brass Funnels and Tunnels

Milestones:
28. Craft **Electron Tubes** *(Iron + Redstone + Gold Nugget)*
29. Craft **Brass Funnels** *(smart item routing into/out of inventories)*
30. Craft **Brass Tunnels** *(round-robin and filtered belt distribution)*

### Chapter 8 — Mechanical Crafters (Tier 7)

**Research:** `brass-age/mechanical-crafters`  
**Requires:** Electronics & Sorting research

Workers involved:
- **Blacksmith** (Lv7): Produces Mechanical Crafter blocks
- **Librarian** (Lv6): Unlocks Crushing Wheels research

Milestones:
31. Craft **Mechanical Crafters** *(Brass Casing + Electron Tube)*
32. Build a **Mechanical Crafter array** *(supports shaped recipes beyond vanilla)*
33. Use the array to craft **Crushing Wheels** *(requires Mechanical Crafting — Andesite Alloy + Planks + Stone; far superior to Millstone)*

### Chapter 9 — Advanced Logistics (Tier 8)

**Research:** `brass-age/advanced-logistics`  
**Requires:** Mechanical Crafters research

Workers involved:
- **Mechanic** (Lv8): Produces Precision Mechanisms, Deployers, and Mechanical Arms
- **Carpenter** (Lv8): Advanced component assembly
- **Blacksmith** (Lv8): Produces core Brass components

Milestones:
34. Craft a **Precision Mechanism** *(made inside a Mechanical Crafter from Brass components)*
35. Craft a **Deployer** *(automates right-click interactions — needs Brass + Electron Tube)*
36. Craft a **Mechanical Arm** *(smart item transfer between any blocks; requires Precision Mechanism)*

### Chapter 10 — Power Mastery (Tier 8-9)

**Research:** `brass-age/power-mastery`  
**Requires:** Advanced Logistics research

Workers involved:
- **Blacksmith** (Lv9): Produces Encased Chain Drive
- **Mechanic** (Lv9): Fine-tunes Rotation Speed Controllers
- **Crusher** (Lv9): Operates Large Water Wheels and advanced processing

Milestones:
37. Craft an **Encased Chain Drive** *(transmit rotation at consistent speed over distance)*
38. Craft a **Rotation Speed Controller** *(dial exact RPM for any machine)*
39. Upgrade to a **Large Water Wheel** *(4 RPM, 512 SU — double a regular wheel)*

### Chapter 11 — Full Automation Finale (Tier 9-10)

**Research:** `brass-age/full-automation`  
**Requires:** Power Mastery research

Workers involved:
- **Blacksmith** (Lv10): Produces advanced components (Spout, etc.)
- **Mechanic** (Lv10): Designs and oversees full automation systems
- **Carpenter** (Lv10): Complex assembly and optimization
- All workers (Lv9-10): Can work within fully automated systems

Milestones:
40. Craft a **Spout** *(fills items with fluid automatically — used for potions, blaze cakes, etc.)*
41. Build a fully automated **ore processing line** *(Crushing Wheels → Fan Washing → storage)*
42. Build an **automated Mechanical Crafter line** *(producing components on demand)*
43. Wire everything together with **Mechanical Arms** — master of the Brass Age

## Recipe Gating by Research

All Create component recipes should be locked behind their corresponding chapter research, using the `research-id` field in crafter recipes. Examples:

```json
{
  "type": "recipe",
  "crafter": "blacksmith_crafting",
  "research-id": "create_colonies:andesite-age/rotational-foundations",
  "inputs": [...],
  "result": {...}
}
```

This ensures players must complete the research prerequisites before workers can craft items.
